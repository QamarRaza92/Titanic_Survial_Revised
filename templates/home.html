About the Titanic Dataset

The Titanic dataset provides detailed information about the passengers who were aboard the Titanic. It includes data on passenger age, gender, class, fare, and whether they survived the disaster. The dataset is widely used for data analysis and machine learning projects.

What You Can Do

  • View Data Visualizations: Explore various charts and graphs that provide insights into the Titanic data.
  • Predict Survival: Use our machine learning model to predict whether a passenger would survive based on their details.
  • Analyze Trends: Investigate trends and patterns in the data, such as the survival rate by class, gender, and age.
